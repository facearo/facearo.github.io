Welcome to the Artist's Block! My name is Imaad Mian and I am the creator of the Artist's Block Podcast. The original podcast was meant to be a platform for youth to discover new passions by hearing from some of their peers who have experience in the arts, community organizations and more! This website is different because it's going to be more about myself: my interests, my qualifications and so forth. And this will hopefully be done completely from scratch :)

What I've learned over my short years of coding is the importance of having a goal in mind. Without a goal for learning, it's hard to figure out what to learn, languages are vast! For this site, here are my main goals and what I want this to site to look like.

Three Main Goals for the Site:

  1. A Resume
  2. Coding and Creative Portfolios
  3. Personal Blog

Now I understand that this entire project could have been done much easier with web tools. Frankly, I don't see the value in letting another service do all the work for you, this is better. Here are a few reasons why I believe coding this myself is a better idea than automation.

Reasons to Code from Scratch:

  • Resume points
  • Creative Freedom
  • More precise process
  • Greater Appreciation

Project completion date: End of Summer 2020
